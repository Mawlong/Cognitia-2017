Arpeggio is the musical event of our TechFest, Cognitia, where one will be given a chance to show his talent on an instrument.

RULES

1. The Participants must know at least an instrument like Guitar, Keyboard,Violin etc.

3. No Karaoke will be allowed.
4. Other College Students will not be allowed to participate.
5. The participant's coming for the audition will have to bring their own Instrument.
