Brick Breaker

ABOUT THE EVENT

  1. The quiz is open to all ( i.e students from any college/university can participate in it).
  2. The quiz will be conducted offline( i.e on paper and pen).
  3. Each participants has to register individually and not in team.
  4. The quiz will be comprising of 30 questions and the participants will be provided 30 minutes for that.
  5. All the questions will be of objective type.
  6. Three(3) marks will be awarded for each correct answer.
  7. One(1) mark will be deducted (negative marking) for each wrong answer.
  8. If a question is not attempted then no awarding/deducting of any marks.
