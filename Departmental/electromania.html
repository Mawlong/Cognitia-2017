Electromania

Description

Will be conducted in 3 rounds

PRELIMS ROUND :

  • Candidates will have to go through a pictorial based quiz in which they will be shown different pictures with clues related to electrical ground , you have to write your answer in provided answer sheet.Maximum of 15 teams will qualify for the next round.
TREASURE HUNT ROUND:
  • As the name suggests -“Hunt For Treasure” which are electrical elements hidden within the college campus for which proper hints will be given.
  • On the basis of electrical circuits created by them (using real circuit elements found) , scores will be given to each teams.
  • As per the obtained score 7 teams will move to the final round.
HOT-SPOT ROUND :
  • TSince its final round , main thrill will be here.
    We are keeping the details of this round a suspense for the participants. But be sure that it will be equally related to the electrical theme as the other events which will be disclosed right at the moment of the event.

Participation

Students from all year can participate in this event.
