Questathon

Description

This event will test your electrical engineering concepts against our objective type questions and analyse how much grip
you have over your knowledge.The questions will be from a variety of concepts and the quiz will be designed keeping in mind that
students from all years having background should be able to participate. The event will proceed in 2 rounds.

Rules

Screening Round:

Participants have to form a team of 2 members each, to appear in the screening round in which a written test containing objective questions will be asked in two levels :

No. of questions in the question paper will be 60 and time allowed to complete them will be thirty minutes.
Of all the teams (including both levels) in the quiz, only six teams will be chosen overall, for the final round of the quiz.

Final Round:

  • Of the six teams ,three teams will be winners and will be given certificates on the basis of 5 rounds of hardcore quizzing.

Participation

Students from all the year can participate.
