The Ultimate Swot

ROUND 1:
  • The participants are tested on the basis of their knowledge and circuit designing and deciphering skills.
  • Participants are required to have basic knowledge of logic circuits (analog and digital).

RULES:

  • 10 groups will be selected for next round.
  • Each group will have 2 members.
ROUND 2:
This round tests the participants knowlege on basic semiconductor physics and electronics devices and circuits. It consists of 20 multiple choice questions based on semiconductor physics. Time provided is 30 minutes.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

  • Accuracy

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

  • 10 groups will be selected for next round.
  • Each group will have 2 members.
  • Any malpractice is strictly treated and is submitted to The Cognitia Disciplinary Committee.
