CATECHISM

DESCRIPTION

Questions (Both Subjective and objective) range from fundamentals of electronics to applied electronics of varying difficulty levels.

PARTICIPATION

A General quiz Open for all students irrespective of their branches and semester. 2 participants per team allowed.

COMPETITION RULES

First Round: Preliminary round in which participants will be asked 20 Questions in overall.
Each questions carries 1 point.
Six groups will be selected for final round.
Final round will be held on the same day.
prizes and certificate will be awarded to top 3 team.
